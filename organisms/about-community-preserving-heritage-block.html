Preserving Our Heritage

SingPost leverages its network of post offices, mySam kiosks and digital platforms, to promote public donations to charities, as well as support partners such as the Singapore Police Force and the Central Narcotics Bureau in their community awareness campaigns.

National stamp issues are made to celebrate historical events and shared memories of yesteryear.

SingPost was a long-time partner and supporter of the former Singapore Philatelic Museum, funding heritage preservation and public education programmes for nearly 25 years, and was awarded Patron of Heritage by the National Heritage Board over the years.
