COVID-19 UPDATES10 JUN 2021

International Postal Services Updates

Share to:

In our continuous efforts to enhance our products and services, we will be making changes to several existing services from 15 October 2020, Thursday.

New Domestic Rates from 15 October 2020

We will be adjusting the rate structures for domestic letterbox package delivery services and doorstep parcel delivery services:

  • Basic Package and Tracked Package will now have flat rates, for items up to a maximum weight of 2kg.
  • Poly M and SmartPac prices will be adjusted and have a weight limit of 2kg for all items.
  • Speedpost Economy will cease; Speedpost Standard will now have a flat rate for parcels weighing up to 30kg.
  • Local Postage
  • Postage-paid Packaging
