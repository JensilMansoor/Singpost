Community Support

Facilitating public donations and supporting community campaigns

SingPost leverages its network of post offices, mySam kiosks and digital platforms, to promote public donations to charities, as well as support partners such as the Singapore Police Force and the Central Narcotics Bureau in their community awareness campaigns.

In FY20219/20, SingPost issued Asia's first charity stamps featuring designs drawn by special needs students from the Movement for the Intellectually Disabled of Singapore (MINDS). The stamps were the first in the region to have their sales proceeds wholly donated to a charity. SingPost matched all donations on a dollar-for-dollar basis, effectively doubling the amount donated.
