Always Adapting

SingPost believes that we are only as successful as our employees, which is why we invest in shaping our workforce to be future-fit.

By offering accessible learning opportunities through e-learning platforms, annual partnerships with SkillsFuture, monthly lunch-and-learn sessions and specially curated On-demand Learning Journeys supporting specific business upskilling needs, we create an agile learning mindset in our workforce to ensure they stay relevant in changing times.

We also remain committed in developing leadership capabilities at the middle management level through our accelerated development programme, iLEAD, and Manager Effectiveness Programmes as we strengthen efforts to grow our own timber, improve our talent bench and support the career aspirations of our employees.
