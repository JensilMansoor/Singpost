Value-Added Services

Advice of Receipt (AR)

With the opt in of the AR service, the recipient will be requested to sign on the AR card and this card will be returned to the sender via Basic mail. This optional service is available at $1.00, on top of the registered fee plus postage.

Note:

  • The availability of the signed AR card is subject to the recipient of the item and the Overseas Postal Administration providing the acknowledgment.
  • Receipt of the AR card by the sender is dependent on the recipient’s Overseas Postal Administration and may take up to 60 days.
