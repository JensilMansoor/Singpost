Our Environmental Policy articulates our primary environmental objectives and commitments around four thrusts

Collaborating with Stakeholders

We aim to work closely with our investor, customers, suppliers, business partners and regulators to establish environmentally sustainable and responsible business practices.

Operational Efficiency

We commit to base our operations on key principles of international environmental management system standards and achieve resource efficiency through the optimization of resources in our value chain.

Fleet Efficiency

We aim to apply innovative solutions that optimize and upgrade our fleets, reduce mileage, explore alternative fuel-saving options and implement initiatives to reduce delivery trips.

Building Efficiency

We are dedicated towards energy efficiency in our sites and facilitites, utilizing alternative energy sources in place of grid electricity, and progressively refurbishing outdated infrastructure.
