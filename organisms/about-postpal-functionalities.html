Functionalities of PostPal

Customisation

Storage modules can be customised to the mail profile of individual blocks. Full letterboxes will be a thing of the past.

Real-time Monitoring

Every PostPal is equipped with sensors that will alert us if masterdoors are left unlocked. CCTVs are installed at every PostPal as well.

Auto-sorting

Each PostPal is able to autosort mail, which greatly minimises misdeliveries, while significantly improving the postman's productivity.

Keyless Convenience

Residents need only scan a QR code generated by the SingPost app to retrieve their mail.

Enhanced Mail Security

Storage modules can be customised to the mail profile of individual blocks. Full letterboxes will be a thing of the past.

Mail Receipt Notifications

Residents no longer need to 'blind-check' their letterboxes, as they will be notified via the SingPost app whenever they receive mail.
