Deposit/Withdraw Cash (POSB/DBS) Rates

POSB/DBS customers can perform basic banking transactions at any post office island wide. Transactions such as cash deposit or withdrawal can now be conveniently performed over the counters at our post offices.

  • Min deposit/withdrawal amount - S$10 per card
  • Max deposit/withdrawal amount - S$5,000 per card

Deposit and withdrawals are in multiples of S$10. We also do not accept cents or coins.
