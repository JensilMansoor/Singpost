Tracked Package is a new and affordable local delivery service for small packages up to 2kg that is delivered to the letterbox within 2 working days.

SMS notifications will be sent to the recipient when the Tracked Package is ready for delivery and when it has been successfully delivered to the letterbox. 24/7 online tracking is also available via SingPost.com or the SingPost app.
